Overview

The Landed Margin Forecasting Tool enables management to assess the impact of adjusting customer margins on the achievable landed margin. Using historical data and freight cost constraints, this tool provides insights for strategic pricing adjustments.

Features

  • Calculates margin based on costs and revenue from historical order data.
  • Uses linear functions to forecast landed margins across various margin buckets.
  • Applies a freight charge limit, adjusting calculations when the charge exceeds 20% of the product order total.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Data Analysis and Forecasting
  • Advanced Excel Functions
  • Process Optimization
  • Project Management
  • Strategic Decision Support
